# Privacy Policy — Hangman

_Last updated: 2026-05-04_

This Privacy Policy describes how the Hangman mobile application ("the App")
handles information.

## What we collect
**We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The App stores the following **only on your device**, in the browser/WebView's
local storage:

- Your best score, highest level reached, win count, and longest streak.

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App removes it.

## Network access
The App requests the `INTERNET` permission solely so the embedded WebView can
load the bundled assets. The App does not connect to any server, analytics
service, or third-party tracker.

## Children's privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13
(or the age of consent in your jurisdiction). Because the App collects no
data at all, this provision is satisfied by design.

## Permissions
The App requests no Android runtime permissions (no camera, microphone,
location, contacts, storage access, etc.).

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes in a future release, the updated version will replace
this file and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
